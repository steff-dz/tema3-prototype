Custom Orders

If you're looking for a custom piece, I'd love to help you out! I love taking people's ideas and needs, and turning them into a working piece of furniture echoing their asthetic. The custom furniture I build is made with the finest quality; it's built to last. So whether you are searching for a desk, bookshelf, or table to fit into your space just right, I'm sure I could hammer it out.

Before you proceed to the order form, please keep in mind that custom work can be costly. Besides paying for hand craftsmanship, the price of custom furniture must also cover the cost of materiels. Certain materiels are more expensive than others, which would factor into your quote.

Additionally, it takes time to make pieces by hand. An estimation of production time will also be sent once the form has been received.

If you have any questions along the way, there's a space to include them at the end! So, follow the button to go ahead and get started!
