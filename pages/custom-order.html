Custom Orders

If you're looking for a custom furniture piece, let me help you out! I love taking a customer's ideas and needs, and creating a suitable piece for them that echoes their asthetic. My pieces are made with top quality material and they are built to last. So, whether you need a shelf to store your treasures, or a desk to fit an awkward space, let me hammer it out for you!

I've provided a form so you can specify what kind of furniture piece you'd like, desired materials, and your budget. Upon form completion, you will receive an email from me with an estimated production time and a quote.

Please keep in mind that there are a lot of factors going into a quote. Creating high quality furniture by hand is costly, and the price is also dependent on the materiels you choose as some are more expensive than others.

Once you're ready, continue onto the form! There will be space on the final page for questions and additonal information.
